University Official Profile

Biography

Professor Li Bingzhao is primarily engaged in teaching courses such as "Mathematical Experiment," "Mathematical Modeling," and "Mathematical Methods in Information Analysis." He actively promotes reforms in university mathematics education and teaching, and guides students in participating in college mathematical modeling competitions. His long-term research focuses on the emerging interdisciplinary integration of mathematics and information science, and he has successfully led 4 projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
