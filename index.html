Welcome

Welcome to the Li Bingzhao Laboratory at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Beijing Institute of Technology. Our research focuses on the emerging interdisciplinary integration of mathematics and information science, including mathematical modeling, statistical data analysis, signal and image processing, fractional Fourier transform, and graph signal processing methods.
